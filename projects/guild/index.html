ZEEPS CASES
Zeeps was a Major Studio Project of mine from my third year at Griffith University. We were asked to build a hypothetical business from scratch; everything from the logo to the products themselves. I decided on redesigning how people use cases (pencil case, makeup bag, iPad case etc). There are literally thousands of different materials that go unused in OP shops for years: so why not reuse them for something that has a chance to be loved like new? Zeeps intended to make the world overall more renewable. Zeeps Yellow was a side assessment that partnered Zeeps up with the Lighthouse Foundation to help spread awareness for homeless youth in Australia.
